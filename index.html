AP Precalculus Formula Guide

Clean, structured formulas for fast studying.

Function Transformation
\( y = a f(b(x-h)) + k \)
Composition
\( (f \circ g)(x)=f(g(x)) \)
Inverse Function
\( f^{-1}(f(x))=x \)
Average Rate of Change
\( \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a} \)
Even Function
\( f(-x)=f(x) \)
Odd Function
\( f(-x)=-f(x) \)
Exponential Growth
\( f(x)=ab^x \)
Exponential Decay
\( f(x)=ab^x,\;0<b<1 \)
Pythagorean Identity
\( \sin^2x+\cos^2x=1 \)
Tangent Identity
\( \tan x=\frac{\sin x}{\cos x} \)
Law of Sines
\( \frac{a}{\sin A}=\frac{b}{\sin B}=\frac{c}{\sin C} \)
Law of Cosines
\( c^2=a^2+b^2-2ab\cos C \)
Arc Length
\( s=r\theta \)
Quadratic Formula
\( x=\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a} \)
Slope
\( m=\frac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1} \)
Distance Formula
\( d=\sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2+(y_2-y_1)^2} \)
Midpoint
\( \left(\frac{x_1+x_2}{2},\frac{y_1+y_2}{2}\right) \)
Arithmetic Sequence
\( a_n=a_1+(n-1)d \)
Geometric Sequence
\( a_n=a_1r^{n-1} \)
Infinite Geometric Sum
\( S=\frac{a_1}{1-r} \)
Log Definition
\( \log_b x=y \Leftrightarrow b^y=x \)
Product Rule
\( \log(ab)=\log a+\log b \)
Natural Log
\( \ln x=\log_e x \)
